Welcome!

My name is Mitch and I am a Machine Learning / Data Scientist who enjoys making unique and fun projects in my free time. This site acts as a a place where I can document and share some of my favorite projects in a more structured format. Feel free to check out my YouTube page or other social media platforms for more projects!

An AI That Orders You A Pizza If You're Sad

To be honest, I'm not sure why people are so scared of robots. I think the good that they can do definitely outweighs the bad. In an attempt to try to show how useful AI can be, I decided to create a robot that can order a pizza for you based on your emotions. Hopefully these robots don’t replace our friends anytime soon!
